# Phase 19: Content Cluster Strategy

## Purpose

Define content clusters and pillar article strategy for AI Product Build Lab.

The goal is to avoid publishing disconnected articles and instead build clear
SEO topic groups connected by internal links.

## Scope

In scope:

- Define content clusters based on the current three content pillars.
- Identify existing published articles that can act as pillar or cluster
  candidates.
- Identify draft articles that can support clusters.
- Identify missing pillar and cluster article opportunities.
- Define internal link direction between pillar and cluster articles.

Out of scope:

- Creating new articles.
- Editing existing article content.
- Changing site UI.
- Changing article URLs or slugs.
- Changing Discord bot code.
- Running VPS deployment.
- Adding SaaS, login, payment, or dashboard features.
